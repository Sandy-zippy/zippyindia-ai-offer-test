## 3. Reel Concepts (10)

### Reel 1: "The Attribution Test"
- **Hook (0-3s):** "Ask your agency this ONE question. If they can't answer, fire them today."
- **Structure:** Sandy on camera, direct address. Asks: "Which channel brought your last 10 paying customers?" Explains why 90% of agencies can't answer this. Shows ZippyScale dashboard briefly. Ends with the gap between spending and knowing.
- **CTA:** "DM 'AUDIT' and we'll answer this for your brand in 48 hours."

### Reel 2: "₹10K/Day Burn Rate"
- **Hook (0-3s):** "This founder was spending ₹10K per day on ads. Making ₹0 back."
- **Structure:** Story format. Sets the scene (₹3L/month ad spend, zero tracking). Shows what we found (wrong audience, no attribution, broken pixel). Shows the fix (3 changes in 1 week). Shows the result (ROAS went from 0.3x to 4.2x).
- **CTA:** "Link in bio for a free growth audit."

### Reel 3: "Agency Red Flags" (Comedy)
- **Hook (0-3s):** "POV: Your marketing agency's monthly report just arrived."
- **Structure:** Sandy opens a fake "report" that's just screenshots of impressions. Reaction shots. Cuts to what a REAL report should look like (revenue attribution, CAC, LTV). Quick comparison: vanity metrics vs. money metrics.
- **CTA:** "Tag your founder friend who needs to see this."

### Reel 4: "The Discovery Call"
- **Hook (0-3s):** "We rejected 7 out of 10 brands last month. Here's why."
- **Structure:** Sandy explains ZippyScale's selectivity. Shows the 3 criteria: ₹5Cr+ revenue, product-market fit confirmed, willingness to track everything. Explains why this protects BOTH sides. Positions scarcity as quality control.
- **CTA:** "Think you qualify? Link in bio."

### Reel 5: "Marketing Math"
- **Hook (0-3s):** "Your agency charges ₹1L/month. They should be making you ₹10L minimum."
- **Structure:** Whiteboard style. Sandy does the math on camera: agency fee vs. expected return. Shows what 10x ROI looks like. Shows what most agencies actually deliver (1x to 2x). Introduces attribution as the unlock.
- **CTA:** "Want us to do this math for your brand? DM 'MATH'."

### Reel 6: "Funnel Autopsy" (Educational)
- **Hook (0-3s):** "This ₹20Cr brand's funnel has 4 holes. Let me show you each one."
- **Structure:** Screen recording walkthrough of a funnel (anonymized). Points out: landing page mismatch, no retargeting, broken form, zero post-purchase flow. Shows fix for each. Reveals potential revenue recovered.
- **CTA:** "We do this for free in our discovery call. Book yours."

### Reel 7: "Boost Post Culture" (Comedy)
- **Hook (0-3s):** "Indian marketing in 2026: Bhai, post boost kar do, leads aa jayenge."
- **Structure:** Skit format. Boss tells intern to "boost the post." Intern boosts random post with ₹500. Boss asks where leads are. Cut to Sandy explaining why boosted posts are the most expensive way to get zero results.
- **CTA:** "Share this with someone who still boosts posts."

### Reel 8: "AI + Attribution Stack"
- **Hook (0-3s):** "We use AI to find money your marketing is hiding from you."
- **Structure:** Sandy shows the concept: AI scans all channels, finds the hidden revenue patterns, reallocates budget automatically. Shows a before/after dashboard. Explains how this saves 15+ hours per week of manual analysis.
- **CTA:** "This is what 'Data Finds Money, AI Multiplies It' means. Link in bio."

### Reel 9: "Client Transformation"
- **Hook (0-3s):** "This founder called us 'expensive.' 90 days later, he called us 'cheap.'"
- **Structure:** Storytelling format. Month 1: skepticism, setup, first dashboard. Month 2: first optimizations, ROAS climbing. Month 3: results that paid for 12 months of fees. Founder testimonial clip or quote overlay.
- **CTA:** "10 spots per quarter. Check if one's open. Link in bio."

### Reel 10: "What Founders Get Wrong"
- **Hook (0-3s):** "₹5Cr+ founders make this SAME marketing mistake. Every. Single. Time."
- **Structure:** Sandy reveals: they hire for tactics (SEO, ads, social) before building a measurement system. It's like driving without a speedometer. Shows the ZippyScale approach: measure first, spend second. Quick framework visual.
- **CTA:** "Follow for more frameworks that save you lakhs."

## 6. Content Repurposing Map

**One Piece Becomes Five**

Take any carousel or reel and follow this map:

### Source: Carousel (e.g., "5 Metrics Your Agency Hides")

| Platform | Format | Adaptation |
|----------|--------|------------|
| **Instagram** | Carousel (10 slides) | Original post. Full slide deck with caption hook and CTA. |
| **LinkedIn** | Text post (long-form) | Rewrite as a personal story/opinion post. "I audited 23 agencies last year. Only 2 could answer these 5 questions..." Lead with a bold statement. No slides needed. |
| **Twitter/X** | Thread (7-10 tweets) | Each metric becomes a tweet. First tweet is the hook. Last tweet links to full carousel or CTA. |
| **WhatsApp** | Broadcast message | "Quick question for you: Can your agency tell you which channel brought your last 10 paying customers? If not, we should talk. [link]" Short, personal, one clear ask. |
| **Email** | Newsletter segment | Subject: "The 5 numbers your agency hopes you never ask for." Body: Story format with the 5 metrics. CTA: Book a free audit. |

### Source: Reel (e.g., "The Attribution Test")

| Platform | Format | Adaptation |
|----------|--------|------------|
| **Instagram** | Reel (60-90 sec) | Original reel with captions, trending audio if relevant. |
| **LinkedIn** | Native video + text | Upload same video natively. Add a 3-line text intro: "I asked 50 agencies one question. 47 couldn't answer." |
| **Twitter/X** | Video tweet + quote thread | Post the video. Quote tweet with the key insight as text for those who won't watch. |
| **WhatsApp** | 30-second cut + message | Trim to the punchiest 30 seconds. Send with context: "Saw this and thought of you. Is your agency tracking this?" |
| **Email** | GIF/thumbnail + link | Use a 3-second GIF as the email hero image. "I made a 60-second video that might save you ₹2L/month. Watch here." |

### Repurposing Workflow

1. **Monday:** Create the core piece (carousel or reel) for Instagram.
2. **Tuesday:** Adapt for LinkedIn (rewrite as personal narrative or opinion).
3. **Wednesday:** Break into Twitter thread.
4. **Thursday:** Cut WhatsApp broadcast version. Send to warm leads.
5. **Friday:** Include in weekly email newsletter.

**Rule:** Every piece must have a trackable CTA. Different UTMs per platform so attribution stays clean.
