## Systemic Issues Across ALL Creatives

1. **MAJOR: Too much dead space at the bottom.** Nearly every creative has 100-200px of unused space below the CTA. This wastes precious ad real estate and makes creatives look unfinished.
2. **MAJOR: Many creatives alternate between dark (#2A2A35) and light (#FFFDF7) backgrounds with no visual richness.** Plain solid backgrounds feel like wireframes, not finished ads. Need textures, gradients, imagery, or patterns.
3. **Repetitive stat formatting.** The "big number + subtitle" layout (e.g., "₹40L+/yr / wasted on busywork") is used in 25+ creatives. It becomes wallpaper by the 5th time someone sees one.
4. **No photography or real imagery anywhere.** All 50 creatives are text + CSS shapes. This is unusual for Meta ads where image-heavy creatives dominate. At minimum, some creatives should incorporate stock photography or custom illustrations.
5. **"Implementation timeline" item missing from pra1** -- list shows only 4 items but headline says businesses get audit content. CTA overlaps the last list item.
6. **Logo is very small** in all creatives. At 1080x1080, the Zippyscale logo in the top-left is tiny. Fine for brand consistency but could be larger for awareness-stage ads.

## Priority Fix List

### Critical (Score < 6):
Fix the **7 creatives at 5.5/10** first. They share a common problem: plain backgrounds (usually white) with nothing but text. They need:
- Background textures, gradients, or subtle patterns
- At least one visual element (chart, icon set, mockup, or illustration)
- CTA moved higher with more visual prominence
- Dead space filled or canvas height reduced

### High Priority:
- **Reduce dead space** on all 50 creatives. Most have 100-300px of unused space below the CTA.
- **Diversify layouts** -- at least 15 creatives use the identical "headline + big stat + body text + CTA" template. Swap some for data visualizations, mockups, or split layouts.
- **Add real imagery** to at least 10 creatives -- stock photography, custom illustrations, or product screenshots.
- **Increase CTA size** across the board. Most CTAs are small and left-aligned. For Meta ads, CTAs should be larger, centered, and higher up.

### Nice to Have:
- Replace emoji icons with proper SVG/design icons in pa8, pra10, ba4
- Add animation hints (CSS would be stripped in static but helps for video variants)
- Create dark/light variants of each creative for A/B testing
