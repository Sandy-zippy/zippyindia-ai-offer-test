## Testing Matrix

| Awareness Level | Test Priority | Budget Split | Audience Size |
|----------------|--------------|-------------|---------------|
| Solution Aware | Highest | 40% | Smallest (warmest) |
| Problem Aware | High | 30% | Medium |
| Unaware | Medium | 20% | Largest (coldest) |
| Brand Aware | Ongoing | 10% | Retargeting pool |

### A/B Testing Plan
1. **Week 1-2:** Test all 3 primary texts per awareness level. Keep best headline/description combo constant.
2. **Week 3-4:** Winner from step 1 stays. Now test headlines against each other.
3. **Week 5+:** Full winner combos. Test creatives (static vs carousel vs video).

### Audience Targeting Notes
- **Unaware:** Broad interests: "Business owners India," "Entrepreneurship," "SME India." Age 28-50. Tier 1+2 cities.
- **Problem Aware:** Interests: "Business automation," "Productivity tools," "HR management." Lookalike of website visitors.
- **Solution Aware:** Keywords/interests: "AI automation," "Business process automation," "RPA." Narrower targeting.
- **Brand Aware:** Custom audience: website visitors (7-30 day window), page engagers, video viewers 50%+.
