# ZippyScale Video Ad Concepts

**Format:** Vertical 9:16 (1080x1920) for Reels/Stories, crop to 1:1 for Feed
**Duration:** 15-30 seconds each
**Brand:** Dark mode aesthetic. Lime #D5EB4B accents. Space Grotesk/Inter/JetBrains Mono.
**Audio:** Background lo-fi/ambient beat. Voiceover where specified. Subtitles mandatory (80% watch muted).

## Video 1: "The Salary Calculator"

**Duration:** 20 seconds
**Style:** Screen recording / UI animation (no talking head)
**Awareness Level:** Unaware / Problem Aware
**UTM:** `&utm_content=video-calculator`

### Hook (0-3 seconds)
**Visual:** Black screen. A calculator app opens. Someone types "15,00,000" (monthly salary bill). Large text overlay appears: "Your salary bill has a leak."
**Audio:** Calculator key tap sounds. No music yet.
**Why it works:** Number + calculator = pattern interrupt. Viewer's brain goes "wait, let me see this."

### Script + Visual Direction

| Time | Visual | Text Overlay | Audio |
|------|--------|-------------|-------|
| 0-3s | Calculator app, typing ₹15,00,000 | "Your salary bill has a leak" | Key tap sounds |
| 3-6s | Zoom into a spreadsheet-style breakdown. 5 roles with salaries. | "Let's find it." | Lo-fi beat starts |
| 6-10s | 3 rows highlight lime. Labels appear: "Data Entry ₹2.8L" "Follow-ups ₹3.2L" "Reports ₹1.5L" | "These roles are doing AI's job" | Beat continues |
| 10-14s | The 3 rows slide out, replaced by automation icons. Total recalculates. | "₹7.5L/month → ₹3.2L/month" | Subtle whoosh on slide |
| 14-17s | Savings counter ticks up: "₹51.6L saved/year" in massive lime JetBrains Mono | "₹51L saved. Same output." | Counter tick sound |
| 17-20s | CTA card: ZippyScale logo, "Free AI Automation Audit", "10 Spots", lime Apply Now button | "Link in bio" | Beat ends |

### End Card (17-20s)
- Background: #0C0C10
- ZippyScale logo centered top
- "Free AI Automation Audit" in Space Grotesk 28px
- "We find your salary leak. ₹0 cost." in Inter 18px
- "Apply Now" lime button
- "Only 10 businesses/month" in JetBrains Mono 14px, muted

### Production Notes
- Can be built entirely in Remotion (React animation). No filming needed.
- Calculator and spreadsheet should look native iOS/macOS for premium feel.
- All transitions: 0.3s ease-out. No flashy effects.
- Subtitles: White Inter SemiBold 20px with dark semi-transparent bg, positioned bottom-center above safe zone.

## Video 2: "A Day in the Life"

**Duration:** 25 seconds
**Style:** Split screen comparison with text overlays
**Awareness Level:** Problem Aware / Solution Aware
**UTM:** `&utm_content=video-dayinlife`

### Hook (0-3 seconds)
**Visual:** Screen splits vertically. Left label: "WITHOUT AI" (red tint). Right label: "WITH AI" (lime tint). Both show an alarm clock hitting 9:00 AM.
**Audio:** Alarm sound, then two contrasting background tones (chaotic left, calm right).
**Why it works:** Split-screen comparisons are the highest-engagement format on Reels. 9 AM = relatable start.

### Script + Visual Direction

| Time | Left Side (Without AI) | Right Side (With AI) | Text Overlay |
|------|----------------------|---------------------|-------------|
| 0-3s | Alarm rings 9:00 AM. Frantic. | Alarm rings 9:00 AM. Calm. | "WITHOUT AI vs WITH AI" |
| 3-6s | Scrolling through 47 WhatsApp messages. Typing replies one by one. | Dashboard shows "47 leads auto-responded. 12 meetings booked." | "9:00 AM: Lead follow-ups" |
| 6-10s | Opening 5 browser tabs. Copying data into spreadsheet. Frowning. | Glancing at auto-generated report on phone. Nodding. | "10:00 AM: Reports" |
| 10-14s | Manually creating invoices. Stack of papers. Calculator out. | Notification: "200 invoices sent. 3 flagged for review." | "11:30 AM: Invoicing" |
| 14-18s | Still at desk. Pizza box. It's 8 PM. Screen shows half-done work. | Closing laptop at 6 PM. Dashboard green: "All tasks complete." | "End of day." |
| 18-22s | Counter: "12 hrs worked. ₹15L/month ops cost." Red. | Counter: "8 hrs worked. ₹6L/month ops cost." Lime. | "Same work. Half the cost." |
| 22-25s | Both sides merge into CTA card | | "Free AI Audit. 10 Spots." |

### End Card (22-25s)
- Split screen merges into single dark bg
- "Which side is your business on?" in Space Grotesk 24px
- "Free AI Automation Audit" in lime
- "Apply Now" button
- ZippyScale logo

### Production Notes
- Left side: Stock footage or Remotion animations of chaotic office work. Warm-dark color grade.
- Right side: Clean UI mockups, dashboard screens, notification popups. Cool-dark with lime accents.
- Pace: Left side is fast-cut and jerky. Right side is smooth and slow. The editing rhythm tells the story.
- Audio: Left has notification dings, keyboard clacking, stress sounds layered. Right has ambient calm, occasional soft notification chime.
- Can mix stock footage (left) with pure Remotion animation (right) for contrast.

**Awareness Level:** Solution Aware / Brand Aware
**UTM:** `&utm_content=video-auditreveal`

**Visual:** Sandy holding up phone/tablet showing a number. Camera is tight on face + device. Text overlay: "We found ₹32 lakhs hiding in this business."
**Audio:** Sandy: "We found 32 lakhs hiding in this business." Direct to camera. No intro, no "hey guys."
**Why it works:** Specific money figure + "hiding" = curiosity gap. No warm-up, straight into the reveal.

### Script + Visual Direction

| Time | Visual | Sandy's Script | Text Overlay |

| 0-3s | Tight shot, Sandy + device | "We found 32 lakhs hiding in this business." | "₹32L Found" |

### End Card (25-30s)
- Sandy moves to side or fades out
- Dark bg with ZippyScale logo
- "Free AI Automation Audit" in Space Grotesk
- "10 Spots/Month | ₹5Cr+ Revenue"

### Production Notes
- **Camera:** iPhone on tripod. Vertical 9:16. Well-lit face (ring light or window light). Background: clean dark wall or ZippyScale branded backdrop.
- **Sandy's delivery:** Direct, confident, fast-paced. No ums. No "so basically." Cut ruthlessly in edit.
- **Screen share:** Pre-built in Figma or Remotion. NOT actual client data. Anonymized but realistic-looking audit dashboard.
- **Wardrobe:** Dark tee or shirt. No busy patterns. Let the lime brand elements pop on screen, not on clothing.

## General Video Production Guidelines

### First 3 Seconds Rule
Every video lives or dies in the first 3 seconds. Rules:
1. Start with a NUMBER (₹32L, ₹40L, ₹5L leak). Numbers stop thumbs.
2. No logos, no intros, no "hey everyone." Start mid-action or mid-statement.
3. Text overlay must appear within 0.5 seconds. Muted viewers need text immediately.

### Subtitle Specs
- Font: Inter SemiBold 20px
- Color: #FAFAFA on #000000 bg at 70% opacity
- Position: Bottom center, 280px from bottom edge (above platform UI)
- Max 2 lines at a time. 15 words max per subtitle block.
- Hindi text: Roman script (transliterated), not Devanagari

### Aspect Ratios
- **Primary:** 9:16 (1080x1920) for Reels, Stories, TikTok
- **Secondary crop:** 1:1 (1080x1080) for Feed. Ensure key text/visuals are in center 1080x1080 safe area.
- **Tertiary crop:** 16:9 (1920x1080) for YouTube pre-roll. Add side padding if needed.
