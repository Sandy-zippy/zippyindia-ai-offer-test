## Brief 1: Unaware — "The Salary Leak"

### Visual Concept
A dark-mode salary breakdown that looks like a real payroll dashboard. One line item glows lime, highlighted as "waste" with a ₹5L figure. The aesthetic is data-visualization meets exposé. The viewer should feel like they're seeing something they weren't supposed to see.

### Key Text Overlay
- **Headline:** Your ₹15L/month salary bill has a ₹5L leak
- **Subhead:** Tasks AI handles in minutes are costing you full-time salaries
- **CTA:** Get Your Free AI Audit →
- **Badge (top-right):** FREE AUDIT | 10 SPOTS

### Color Palette
- Background: #0C0C10 (near-black)
- Cards/tables: #1E1E24 with #2E2E36 borders
- Highlight/accent: #D5EB4B (lime) for the "leak" row
- Text: #FAFAFA (primary), #9CA3AF (secondary)
- Numbers: JetBrains Mono in lime

### Format Specs

**1080x1080 (Feed)**
- Top 20%: ZippyScale logo (small, top-left) + "FREE AUDIT" badge (top-right)
- Middle 50%: Salary breakdown table. 5 rows of job roles with monthly cost. Row 3-4 highlighted in lime showing "automatable" label. Total leak amount in large JetBrains Mono.
- Bottom 30%: Headline in Space Grotesk 28px. CTA button (lime bg, dark text, rounded).
- Safe zone: 14px padding all sides for feed crop.

**1080x1920 (Story/Reel)**
- Top 15%: Logo + badge
- Middle 55%: Salary table, stacked vertically. Each row is a full-width card. Automatable rows pulse lime.
- Bottom 30%: Headline (Space Grotesk 32px), subhead (Inter 18px), CTA button, swipe-up prompt.
- Safe zone: Top 250px and bottom 250px kept text-free for UI overlays.

**1200x628 (Link Ad)**
- Left 55%: Salary table (compact, 4 rows max). Leak row in lime.
- Right 45%: Headline stacked vertically. CTA button. ZippyScale logo bottom-right.
- Safe zone: 20px padding. Headline must be readable at thumbnail size.

### Do/Don't
- DO: Use real-looking data (₹ amounts in JetBrains Mono). Make it feel like an actual dashboard screenshot.
- DO: Keep the lime highlight to ONE element. Don't lime-wash everything.
- DO: Ensure ₹5L figure is the largest text element after the headline.
- DON'T: Use stock photos. This is a data-driven creative.
- DON'T: Add more than 20% text coverage (Meta's old guideline, still best practice).
- DON'T: Use gradients. Flat, clean, dark-mode aesthetic only.

## Brief 2: Problem Aware — "Hiring Won't Fix It"

### Visual Concept
Split-screen comparison. Left side: "Before" showing a growing team org chart with mounting costs (red-tinted, chaotic). Right side: "After" showing a lean team with automation nodes connecting workflows (lime-tinted, clean). The contrast should be visceral.

### Key Text Overlay
- **Headline:** Hiring more people won't fix your business
- **Subhead:** The bottleneck is process, not people
- **CTA:** Get Your Free AI Audit →
- **Data point:** "Avg savings: ₹28L/year"

### Color Palette
- Background: #0C0C10
- "Before" side: Slight warm tint (#1a1412 bg), costs in #EF4444 (red)
- "After" side: Slight lime tint (#12140c bg), savings in #D5EB4B
- Divider: Thin vertical line #2E2E36
- Text: #FAFAFA primary

### Format Specs

**1080x1080 (Feed)**
- Top 15%: Headline in Space Grotesk 26px, centered
- Middle 60%: Split down the center. Left: org chart icons (stick figures) multiplying, cost counter in red climbing. Right: Clean workflow diagram with automation nodes, cost counter in lime going down. Labels "BEFORE" / "AFTER" in JetBrains Mono.
- Bottom 25%: Subhead, data point ("Avg savings: ₹28L/year" in lime JetBrains Mono), CTA button.

**1080x1920 (Story/Reel)**
- Top half: "BEFORE" scene. Crowded team, rising costs. Dark warm tone.
- Bottom half: "AFTER" scene. Clean automation flow. Dark cool tone with lime accents.
- Overlay: Headline at the split point. CTA at bottom.

**1200x628 (Link Ad)**
- Left 50%: "Before" with org chart chaos and red cost figure
- Right 50%: "After" with automation flow and lime savings figure
- Headline underneath both halves, centered. CTA right-aligned.

### Do/Don't
- DO: Make the contrast dramatic. Before should feel overwhelming, After should feel calm.
- DO: Use iconographic people (simple shapes), not photos or illustrations.
- DO: Show specific numbers (₹15L → ₹9L monthly ops cost).
- DON'T: Make "Before" look insulting. It should feel relatable, not mocking.
- DON'T: Overcrowd the "After" side. Less is more. That's the point.
- DON'T: Use arrows or "vs" text. The split-screen speaks for itself.

## Brief 3: Solution Aware — "The Free Audit"

### Visual Concept
A mockup of the actual audit deliverable. Show a premium-looking document/dashboard with the ZippyScale brand, displaying an automation roadmap with process names, savings figures, and priority rankings. It should look valuable enough that the viewer thinks "I need to see MY version of this."

### Key Text Overlay
- **Headline:** Free AI Automation Audit
- **Subhead:** Custom roadmap for ₹5Cr+ businesses
- **CTA:** Apply Now — 10 Spots Only →
- **Qualifier:** ₹5Cr+ Revenue | 15+ Team | Growth Stage

### Color Palette
- Background: #0C0C10
- Document mockup: #1E1E24 bg with lime accent bars
- Key metrics: #D5EB4B (lime) in JetBrains Mono
- Qualification badge: #FFFDF7 (cream) text on #2E2E36 bg
- CTA button: #D5EB4B bg, #0C0C10 text

### Format Specs

**1080x1080 (Feed)**
- Top 10%: "FREE AI AUTOMATION AUDIT" in Space Grotesk 24px, centered
- Middle 65%: Tilted document mockup (3-5 degree rotation for depth). Document shows: company name redacted, 4-5 process rows with automation potential %, annual savings figure at bottom in large lime text. ZippyScale logo watermark on document.
- Bottom 25%: Qualification criteria in small Inter text. CTA button full-width.

**1080x1920 (Story/Reel)**
- Top 20%: Headline + "Only 10 Spots" badge
- Middle 50%: Document mockup, straight-on, filling width. Key data points highlighted with lime underlines.
- Bottom 30%: Qualification criteria, CTA button, "Swipe up to apply" prompt.

**1200x628 (Link Ad)**
- Left 40%: Headline stacked. "Free AI" in lime, "Automation Audit" in white. Subhead underneath.
- Right 60%: Document mockup at slight angle. Savings figure (₹32L/year) prominently visible.
- Bottom bar: Qualification criteria + CTA button.

### Do/Don't
- DO: Make the document mockup look premium. Think McKinsey report meets dark-mode SaaS dashboard.
- DO: Include realistic-looking (but anonymized) data. Process names like "Invoice Processing," "Lead Qualification."
- DO: Show a specific savings number on the document (₹32L/year) to anchor value.
- DON'T: Make the mockup too detailed. Viewer should want to apply to see the full thing.
- DON'T: Use light-mode for the document. Keep everything dark-mode consistent.
- DON'T: Forget the qualification criteria. It's a filter AND a status signal.

## Brief 4: Brand Aware / Retargeting — "Spots Filling"

### Visual Concept
A minimal progress bar showing "7 of 10 spots filled." The bar is lime on dark, partially filled. Below it, a single testimonial-style data point from a recent audit. Maximum urgency with minimum visual noise. The viewer already knows the offer; this is a nudge, not a pitch.

### Key Text Overlay
- **Headline:** 3 Spots Left for This Month's Free Audit
- **Subhead:** A ₹7Cr business just saved ₹32L/year from theirs
- **CTA:** Apply Now →
- **Counter:** 7/10 SPOTS FILLED (with progress bar)

### Color Palette
- Background: #0C0C10
- Progress bar fill: #D5EB4B (lime)
- Progress bar empty: #2E2E36
- Urgency text: #FFFDF7 (cream)
- CTA button: #D5EB4B bg, #0C0C10 text

### Format Specs

**1080x1080 (Feed)**
- Top 20%: ZippyScale logo + "RETARGETING" internal label (won't show in ad, for team reference)
- Middle 30%: Large "7/10" in JetBrains Mono 72px, lime. Progress bar underneath (70% filled, lime).
- Middle 25%: "Spots filled for March's Free AI Audit" in Space Grotesk 22px
- Bottom 25%: Social proof line in Inter 16px (italic): "Last audit: ₹7Cr business → ₹32L/year in identified savings." CTA button.

**1080x1920 (Story/Reel)**
- Top 30%: "You visited our page." in Space Grotesk 28px. Pause beat.
- Middle 40%: Progress bar (large, horizontal, 70% lime fill). "7 of 10 spots filled" in JetBrains Mono.
- Bottom 30%: Social proof data point. CTA button. "Tap to apply" prompt.

**1200x628 (Link Ad)**
- Left 50%: "3 Spots Left" in Space Grotesk 36px. Progress bar underneath.
- Right 50%: Social proof stat + CTA button.
- Ultra clean. Maximum 4 text elements total.

### Do/Don't
- DO: Keep it minimal. One message: spots are running out.
- DO: Use the progress bar as the hero visual. It's simple and effective.
- DO: Include one specific data point for social proof. Real > vague.
- DON'T: Re-explain the offer. They've seen the landing page.
- DON'T: Use countdown timers (feels scammy for this audience).
- DON'T: Stack multiple social proof points. One strong one beats three weak ones.

## General Design Rules (All Creatives)

### Typography Hierarchy
1. **Data/Numbers:** JetBrains Mono, Bold, Lime #D5EB4B — always the most prominent
2. **Headlines:** Space Grotesk, SemiBold, White #FAFAFA — second-most prominent
3. **Body/Subhead:** Inter, Regular, Light gray #9CA3AF — supporting
4. **CTA Text:** Inter, SemiBold, Near-black #0C0C10 on lime button

### Logo Usage
- Always use SVG source from `/Users/sandy/zippyscale-landing/brand-assets/`
- Minimum size: 80px width on 1080px canvas
- Position: top-left or bottom-right, never centered
- Clear space: minimum 20px around logo

### CTA Button Spec
- Background: #D5EB4B (lime)
- Text: #0C0C10 (near-black), Inter SemiBold 16px
- Padding: 12px 32px
- Border radius: 8px
- No drop shadow. No gradient.

### Safe Zones
- **Feed (1080x1080):** 14px all sides
- **Story (1080x1920):** No critical text in top 250px or bottom 250px (platform UI)
- **Link Ad (1200x628):** 20px all sides. Right 100px may be cropped on some placements.

### File Naming Convention
```
zs-{awareness}-{format}-v{version}.{ext}
```
Examples:
- `zs-unaware-feed-v1.png`
- `zs-solution-story-v2.png`
- `zs-retarget-link-v1.png`
