# ZippyScale Copy Framework

## The 8-Line Formula

Every ZippyScale ad creative follows this exact structure:

| # | Element | Purpose | Max Length |
|---|---------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | **CONTROVERSY** | Pattern interrupt. Contrarian belief that stops the scroll. | 1 line |
| 2 | **PAIN #1** | First pain point. Specific, visceral, recognizable. | 1 line |
| 3 | **PAIN #2** | Stack the second pain. Twist the knife. "And on top of that..." | 1 line |
| 4 | **BRIDGE** | Transition to hope. "Here's the thing..." or "But what if..." | 1 line |
| 5 | **SOLUTION** | What ZippyScale does differently. One sentence. | 1-2 lines |
| 6 | **PLEASURE #1** | First outcome they'll experience. Future-pace. | 1 line |
| 7 | **PLEASURE #2** | Second outcome. Make them feel the relief. | 1 line |
| 8 | **CTA** | Clear, specific next step. One action. | 1 line |

### Rules
- **Headline:** Under 125 characters. Must contain the CONTROVERSY or PAIN #1.
- **Body:** Under 500 characters total. All 8 elements packed in.
- **Language:** Hindi-English mix where it feels natural. Not forced. Think "how a founder talks to another founder over chai."
- **No jargon:** Never say "digital transformation," "leverage," "synergy," or "holistic." Say what it actually does.
- **Specificity wins:** Numbers > adjectives. "47 minutes" beats "saves time." "₹12L/year" beats "reduces costs."
- **One avatar per ad:** Each ad speaks to ONE avatar. Never try to speak to everyone.

## Ad Format Guidelines

| Format | Headline | Body | Best For |
|--------|----------|------|----------|
| **Meta Feed** | 125 chars max | 500 chars body + 125 chars link description | Stages 1-3 |
| **Meta Stories/Reels** | 80 chars max | On-screen text only (4-5 lines) | Stages 1-2 |
| **LinkedIn Feed** | 100 chars max | 600 chars body | Stage 3-4 (Arjun) |
| **YouTube Pre-roll** | N/A (video) | First 5 sec = controversy hook | Stage 1 |
| **Google Search** | 30 chars headline x3 | 90 chars description x2 | Stages 3-4 |
| **Retargeting (all)** | 90 chars max | 300 chars max | Stage 5 |
