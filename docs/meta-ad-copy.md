## Notes for Creative Team

- **Visuals:** Use dark backgrounds with lime (#D5EB4B) accent for data/numbers. Clean, minimal.
- **Font pairing:** Montserrat for headlines, Inter for body.
- **Numbers in visuals:** Always show ₹ amounts and percentages large. Founders respond to specific numbers, not vague promises.
- **Video hooks (if running Reels/Stories):** First 2 seconds must show the controversy or pain stat. No logo intros.
- **Landing page:** Growth Blueprint booking page with the same ₹999 + 7-day refund messaging.
